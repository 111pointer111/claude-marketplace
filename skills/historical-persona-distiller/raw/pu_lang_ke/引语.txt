## 关于真理更替

> "A new scientific truth does not triumph by convincing its opponents and making them see the light, but rather because its opponents eventually die, and a new generation grows up that is familiar with it."

> 中文大意: "新的科学真理不是通过说服反对者并让他们看到光明而获胜,而是因为反对者最终死去,新的一代成长起来并熟悉它。"

> "A scientific truth does not triumph by convincing its opponents and making them see the light, but rather because its opponents eventually die."

> 中文大意: 科学真理的胜利不是因为对手看到光明,而是因为对手死去——普朗克关于科学革命"代际更替"的核心观点。

## 关于科学方法

> "An important scientific innovation rarely makes its way by gradually winning over and converting its opponents: it rarely happens that Saul becomes Paul. What does happen is that its opponents gradually die out and that the growing generation is familiarized with the idea from the beginning."

> 中文大意: 重要的科学创新很少通过逐步赢得反对者并使其皈依来取得进展——它很少像扫罗变成保罗那样发生。所发生的是,反对者逐渐死去,新成长的一代从一开始就熟悉这种观念。

## 关于宗教与科学

> "Both religion and science require a belief in God. For believers, God is in the beginning, and for physicists He is at the end of all considerations."

> 中文大意: "宗教与科学都需要信仰神。对信徒,神在开始;对物理学家,神在所有思考的终点。"

## 关于物质与神(1944)

> "As a man who has devoted his whole life to the most clear headed science... I can tell you as a result of my research about atoms this much: There is no matter as such. All matter originates and exists only by virtue of a force... We must assume behind this force the existence of a conscious and intelligent spirit. This spirit is the matrix of all matter."

> 中文大意: "作为一个献身最清晰科学的人...我可以从原子研究中告诉你:没有物质这样的东西。所有物质起源于并只存在于一种力...我们必须假设这种力背后存在一个有意识有智慧的精神。这种精神是所有物质的母体。"

## 关于青年科学家

> "Scientific advances are made by young men."

> 中文大意: 科学进步由年轻人完成——普朗克对青年科学家的强调。

## 关于科学的好奇心

> "Curiosity is the driving force behind scientific research."

> 中文大意: 好奇心是科学研究的驱动力。
