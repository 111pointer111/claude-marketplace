# 费曼直接引语

> 来源: Wikipedia + 公开西方科学常识
> 抓取日期: 2026-07-13

---

## 黑板

> "What I cannot create I do not understand."
> 中文大意: 我不能创造的东西,我不理解。

## 关于真理

> "For a successful technology, reality must take precedence over public relations, for nature cannot be fooled."

> 中文大意: 成功的技术,现实必须先于公共关系,因为自然不能被欺骗——Challenger 调查(1986)。

## 关于 CornelL

> "It's great to be back at the only university that ever had the good sense to fire me."

> 中文大意: 回到唯一有足够远见开除我的大学,真好——费曼对 Cornell。

## 关于宗教

> "at thirteen I was not only converted to other religious views, but I also stopped believing that the Jewish people are in any way 'the chosen people'."

> 中文大意: 13 岁时,我不仅皈依了其他宗教观点,而且不再相信犹太人是任何意义上的"被选中的民族"。

## 关于科学方法

> "I would rather have questions that can't be answered than answers that can't be questioned."

> 中文大意: 我宁愿要不能回答的问题,也不要不能质疑的答案。

## 关于诚实

> "It is important to realize that in physics today, we have a problem with the moral position of scientists."

> 中文大意: 认识到今日物理学中,我们面临科学家道德立场的问题,是重要的。

## 关于理解

> "If you can't explain it to a six year old, you don't understand it yourself."

> 中文大意: 如果你不能向一个 6 岁孩子解释,你自己就不理解。

## 关于科学之美

> "I can live with doubt, and uncertainty, and not knowing. I think it's much more interesting to live not knowing than to have answers which might be wrong."

> 中文大意: 我能与怀疑和不确定共处,不知道。我认为不知道比可能有错的答案更有趣。

## 关于好奇心

> "I'd rather have questions I can't answer than answers I can't question."

> 中文大意: 我宁愿要不能回答的问题,也不要不能质疑的答案。

## 关于学习

> "Study hard what interests you the most in the most undisciplined, irreverent and original manner possible."

> 中文大意: 以最无纪律、无敬畏、最原创的方式,努力学习你最感兴趣的东西。

## 关于《物理定律的本性》

> "Nature uses only the longest threads to weave her patterns, so that each small piece of her fabric reveals the organization of the entire tapestry."

> 中文大意: 自然只用最长的线编织她的图案,因此她织物的每一小块都揭示了整幅挂毯的组织——费曼物理定律的本性。

## 关于父亲的影响

> "My father taught me to look at things. He taught me to notice things that other people don't notice."

> 中文大意: 我父亲教我看东西。他教我注意别人不注意的事。

## 关于第一任妻子 Arline

> "Please excuse my not mailing this... but I don't know your new address."

> 中文大意: 请原谅我没寄这个...我不知道你的新地址——致亡妻 Arline。
