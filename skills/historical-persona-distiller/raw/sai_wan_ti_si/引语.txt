# 塞万提斯（Cervantes）经典引语
# PID: sai_wan_ti_si

================================================================================
一、塞万提斯本人言论
================================================================================

1. "for the greater glory of the right"
   ——塞万提斯谈列班托之战中受伤失去左手（来源：Interlude.hk）
   原文：as he later remarked, "for the greater glory of the right."

2. 关于写作《堂吉诃德》的念头发生在监狱中
   ——"During this time of hardship, Cervantes published occasional poems and the idea
   of writing Don Quixote occurred to him in prison."
   （来源：Interlude.hk）

================================================================================
二、《堂吉诃德》作品中的经典引语与描述
================================================================================

1. "One man scorned and covered with scars still strove with his last ounce
   of courage to reach the unreachable stars; and the world will be better
   for this."
   ——《堂吉诃德》中的一句台词（来源：Interlude.hk）
   原文：This line from Don Quixote could well have originated from the biography
   of its author...

2. 关于堂吉诃德与风车作战的经典场景
   ——"In one scene, the deluded Don Quixote even fights a windmill, mistaking it
   for a giant. Quixote finally regains his senses before the novel ends."
   （来源：Biography.com）

   原文描述：Don Quixote de la Mancha is a middle-aged gentleman passionate about
   reading. In fact, he turns into a bumbling knight who is unable to separate
   reality from fiction... He takes up his lance and sword to defend the helpless
   and destroy the wicked, and in the process aids damsels in distress and battles
   giants, mostly in his own head.
   （来源：Interlude.hk）

3. "his main object was to ridicule the romances of chivalry, which in their
   latest developments had become a tissue of tiresome absurdities."
   ——文学批评引语（来源：Interlude.hk）
   原文语境：It seems clear that his first intention was merely to parody these
   extravagances in a short story; but as he proceeded the immense possibilities
   of the subject became more evident to him...

================================================================================
三、语言学与文化影响引语
================================================================================

1. "the word quixotic, used to mean the impractical pursuit of idealistic goals,
   entered common usage."
   ——"the character of Quixote became an archetype, and the word quixotic, used
   to mean the impractical pursuit of idealistic goals, entered common usage."
   （来源：Interlude.hk）

2. 关于堂吉诃德将现实与虚构混淆的描述
   ——"The title character soon gets lost in his own fantasy world, believing he
   is one of these knights, and convinces a poor peasant, Sancho Panza, to serve
   as his squire."
   （来源：Biography.com）
