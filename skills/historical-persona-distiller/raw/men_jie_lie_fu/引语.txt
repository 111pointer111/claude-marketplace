# 门捷列夫引语

## 直接引语（原文/英译）

### 引语1: 关于做梦预见周期表
"I saw in a dream a table where all elements fell into place as required. Awakening, I immediately wrote it down on a piece of paper, only in one place did a correction later seem necessary."
— Mendeleev, as quoted by Inostrantzev, 来源: Wikipedia

### 引语2: 周期律陈述（1869年3月6日）
"Elements arranged according to the value of their atomic weights present a clear periodicity of properties."
— 门捷列夫，向俄罗斯化学学会的报告，来源: Britannica

### 引语3: 关于科学研究
"Mendeleev was raised as an Orthodox Christian, his mother encouraging him to 'patiently search divine and scientific truth'."
— Wikipedia引用门捷列夫母亲对他的教育，来源: Wikipedia

### 引语4: 关于水的化合物研究
门捷列夫1865年的博士论文题目："On the Combinations of Water with Alcohol"（《论水与酒精的化合物》），来源: Wikipedia

### 引语5: 关于元素性质与原子重量
"The magnitude of the atomic weight determines the character of the element, just as the magnitude of the molecule determines the character of a compound body."
— 门捷列夫，周期律核心论述，来源: Wikipedia

### 引语6: 关于元素扩散
"The elements which are the most widely diffused have small atomic weights."
— 门捷列夫，周期律论述，来源: Wikipedia

### 引语7: 关于未知元素的预测
"We must expect the discovery of many yet unknown elements — for example, two elements, analogous to aluminium and silicon, whose atomic weights would be between 65 and 75."
— 门捷列夫，预测未知元素，来源: Wikipedia

### 引语8: 关于周期表预言
"Certain characteristic properties of elements can be foretold from their atomic weights."
— 门捷列夫，来源: Wikipedia

## 后世评述引语

### 评述1: 元素周期律的意义
"His most brilliant achievement was his discovery of the periodic law of the elements. By arranging the elements in order of their atomic weights, Mendeleev created a systematic framework that revealed relationships between elements that had never been apparent before."
— Britannica

### 评述2: 关于预言的验证
"The fact that he was able to predict the properties of elements that had not yet been discovered demonstrates the power of his insight."
— Britannica

### 评述3: 门捷列夫的国际声誉
"By the time Mendeleev died in 1907, he enjoyed international recognition and had received distinctions and awards from many countries."
— Britannica

### 评述4: 《化学原理》的影响
"Osnovy khimii (The Principles of Chemistry) became a classic, running through many editions and many translations."
— Britannica

### 评述5: 门捷列夫的研究精神
"When Mendeleev began to compose the chapter on the halogen elements at the end of the first volume, he compared the properties of this group of elements to those of the group of alkali metals such as sodium. Within these two groups of dissimilar elements, he discovered similarities in the progression of atomic weights, and he wondered if other groups of elements exhibited similar properties."
— Britannica

### 评述6: 关于发现的过程
"Thus, in his effort to make sense of the extensive knowledge that already existed of the chemical and physical properties of the chemical elements and their compounds, Mendeleev discovered the periodic law."
— Britannica

### 评述7: Wikipedia综合评价
"He used the periodic law not only to correct the then-accepted properties of some known elements, such as the valence and atomic weight of uranium, but also to predict the properties of three elements that were yet to be discovered (germanium, gallium, and scandium)."
— Wikipedia

### 评述8: Wikipedia - 门捷列夫的信仰转变
"His son Ivan would later inform that Mendeleev had departed from the Church and embraced a form of 'romanticized deism'."
— Wikipedia

### 评述9: Wikipedia - 早年经历
"Unfortunately for the family's financial well-being, his father became blind and lost his teaching position. His mother was forced to work and she restarted her family's abandoned glass factory."
— Wikipedia

### 评述10: Wikipedia - 海德堡时期
"Instead of working closely with the prominent chemists of the university, including Robert Bunsen, Emil Erlenmeyer, and August Kekulé, he set up a laboratory in his own apartment."
— Wikipedia

### 评述11: 卡尔斯鲁厄会议的重要性
"In later years Mendeleev would especially remember a paper circulated by the Italian chemist Stanislao Cannizzaro that clarified the notion of atomic weights."
— Britannica

### 评述12: 关于门捷列夫的教育工作
"He became professor of general chemistry in 1867 and continued to teach there until 1890."
— Britannica

### 评述13: 门捷列夫获奖情况
"Mendeleev received the Davy Medal in 1882, the Faraday Lectureship Prize in 1889, was elected Foreign Member of the Royal Society in 1892, and received the Copley Medal in 1905."
— Wikipedia (Quick Facts)

### 评述14: 门捷列夫与塞尔斯堡会议
"In September 1860 he attended the International Chemistry Congress in Karlsruhe, convened to discuss such crucial issues as atomic weights, chemical symbols, and chemical formulas."
— Britannica

### 评述15: 关于门捷列夫的印刷工厂家族
"Maria Kornilieva came from a well-known family of Tobolsk merchants, founders of the first Siberian printing house who traced their ancestry to Yakov Korniliev, a 17th-century posad man turned wealthy merchant."
— Wikipedia
