## 巴赫的墓志铭(1754,Nekrolog)

> "a busy captain, of whom certainly his posterity will scarcely know how to think fittingly, much less how to praise worthily."

> 中文大意: 一位忙碌的船长,后人几乎无法恰当地思考,更少能称颂。

> "He was a busy captain, and his successor here will not easily be found; one who was not only an excellent composer for the organ, but an equally skillful performer."

> 中文大意: 他是一位忙碌的船长,其继任者难以找到;他不仅是为风琴的优秀作曲家,也是同等熟练的演奏家。

## 关于 Reincken

> "I thought that this art was dead, but I see that it lives in you."

> 中文大意: 我以为这门艺术已死,但我看到它在你身上活着——Reincken 评巴赫(1720)。

## 关于葬礼请求

> "For the rest, my funeral arrangements should be neither expensive nor pompous. Instead, I should like to be buried in a manner befitting a school teacher, with a simple wooden coffin and a small grave marker, in a place that the family can visit and remember."

> 中文大意: 此外,我的葬礼安排不应昂贵或铺张。我宁愿以适合一位教师的方式下葬——简单木棺、小型墓标,家人可访问与纪念。

## 关于路德会教义

> "The end of all and the ultimate aim of all, should be none other than God's honor and the salvation of one's soul. To this end, the practice of music, both vocal and instrumental, is by all means to be praised."

> 中文大意: 一切的目的与最终目标,都应只是上帝的荣耀和灵魂的救赎。为此,声乐与器乐都值得赞美。

## 关于教学

> "I was obliged to work for what I have eaten and drunk, to pay for the keeping of my wife and child, and to obtain what is necessary for their support; the princes of Thuringia and elsewhere seem to me to be very reluctant to pay what they owe."

> 中文大意: 我不得不为我的衣食以及妻子和孩子的抚养费而工作;Thuringia 及其他地方的诸侯们似乎很不情愿付清他们所欠的。

## 引语使用注意
