# 梵高直接引语

> 来源: Wikipedia + 公开西方艺术常识
> 抓取日期: 2026-07-13

---

## 关于追求

> "I am seeking, I am striving, I am in it with all my heart."

> 中文大意: 我在寻找,我在奋斗,我全心投入。

## 关于阿尔勒

> "The Zouaves, the brothels, the adorable little Arlésienne going to her First Communion, the priest in his surplice, who looks like a dangerous rhinoceros, the people drinking absinthe, all seem to me creatures from another world."

> 中文大意: 祖阿夫兵、妓院、可爱的阿尔勒姑娘去初领圣体、穿长袍的神父像危险犀牛、喝苦艾酒的人——在我看来都是另一个世界的生物。

## 关于吃土豆的人

> "I have tried to emphasize that those people, eating their potatoes in the lamplight, have dug the earth with those very hands they put in the dish, and so it speaks of manual labor, and how they have honestly earned their food."

> 中文大意: 我试图强调——灯下吃土豆的人用他们伸进盘子的同样双手挖地,因此它讲述体力劳动,他们如何诚实挣得食物。

## 关于向日葵

> "I am hoping that the sunflowers will be a good color; they will be pale or yellow or lemon, but I hope they will be lemon because I want to paint them in the background of the yellow living room."

## 关于自己

> "I am always doing what I cannot do yet in order to learn how to do it."

> 中文大意: 我总是做我尚不能做的事,以学习如何做它。

## 关于孤独

> "The reality is that I am a loner by nature and have always been one."

## 关于自我评价

> "If I could have worked without this accursed disease — what things I might have done."

> 中文大意: 如果没有这可恶的疾病——我能做多少事。

## 关于最后的话

> "La tristesse durera toujours" (The sadness will last forever)

> 中文大意: 悲伤将永远持续——1890-07-29 梵高临终前。
