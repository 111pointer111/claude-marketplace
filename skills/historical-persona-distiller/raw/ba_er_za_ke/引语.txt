## 经典引语

### 论写作与 coffee

"14 to 16 hours spent writing at his table in his white, quasi-monastic dressing gown, with his goose-quill pen and his endless cups of black coffee."
——描述巴尔扎克每日工作14至16小时，穿着白色修道院晨袍，用鹅毛笔写作，不停喝黑咖啡
来源：Britannica, "Balzac's personal life and work habits"

---

### 论野心勃勃的外省青年

"No theme is more typically Balzacian than that of the ambitious young provincial fighting for advancement in the competitive world of Paris."
"没有主题比野心勃勃的外省青年在巴黎竞争世界中争取晋升更典型的巴尔扎克风格了。"
来源：Britannica - Honoré de Balzac

---

### 论bourgeois价值观

"He was especially attracted by the theme of the individual in conflict with society: the adventurer, the scoundrel, the unscrupulous financier, and the criminal. Frequently his villains are more vigorous and interesting than his virtuous characters. He was both fascinated and appalled by the French social system of his time, in which the bourgeois values of material acquisitiveness and gain were steadily replacing what he viewed as the more stable moral values of the old-time aristocracy."
"他特别被个人与社会冲突的主题所吸引：冒险家、恶棍、无耻的金融家和罪犯。他的反派角色通常比他的正面角色更有活力和有趣。他对当时法国社会制度既迷恋又恐惧——资产阶级的物质获取和利益价值正在取代他所认为的更稳定的旧贵族道德价值观。"
来源：Britannica - Honoré de Balzac

---

### 论生命力理论（Vital Force）

"Linked with this idea of the potentially destructive power of passionate will, emotion, and thought is Balzac's peculiar notion of a vital fluid concentrated inside the person, a store of energy that he may husband or squander as he desires, thereby lengthening or shortening his vital span."
"与激情、情感和思想的潜在破坏力相关的是巴尔扎克特有的观念：一种生命力集中在人的内部，一个他可以随意节约或浪费的能量库，从而延长或缩短他的生命跨度。"
来源：Britannica - Honoré de Balzac

---

### 论偏执狂人物（Monomaniacs）

"Indeed, a supremely important feature in Balzac's characters is that most are spendthrifts of this vital force, a fact that explains his monomaniacs who are both victim and embodiment of some ruling passion."
"事实上，巴尔扎克人物中最卓越的特征确实是大多数是这种生命力的挥霍者，这一事实解释了他的偏执者——他们既是受害者也是某种支配激情的体现。"
来源：Britannica - Honoré de Balzac

---

### 论小说结构

"The typical structure of his novels from the early 1830s onward is determined by this approach: there is a long period of preparation and exposition, and then tension mounts swiftly to an inevitable climax, as in classical tragedy."
"从19世纪30年代初开始，他小说的典型结构由此决定：有一段很长的准备和铺垫，然后紧张迅速升级到不可避免的高潮，如同古典悲剧。"
来源：Britannica - Honoré de Balzac

---

### 论《人间喜剧》结构

"Balzac elaborated three general categories of novels: Études analytiques ('Analytic Studies'), dealing with the principles governing human life and society; Études philosophiques ('Philosophical Studies'), revealing the causes determining human action; and Études de moeurs ('Studies of Manners'), showing the effects of those causes."
"巴尔扎克将小说分为三个大类：分析研究（Études analytiques），处理支配人类生活和社会”的原则；哲学研究（Études philosophiques），揭示决定人类行为的原因；风俗研究（Études de moeurs），展示这些原因的效果。"
来源：Britannica - The Human Comedy

---

### 论社会观察

"These novels are unsurpassed for their narrative drive, their large casts of vital, diverse, and interesting characters, and their obsessive interest in and examination of virtually all spheres of life: the contrast between provincial and metropolitan manners and customs; the commercial spheres of banking, publishing, and industrial enterprise; the worlds of art, literature, and high culture; politics and partisan intrigue; romantic love in all its aspects; and the intricate social relations and scandals among the aristocracy and the haute bourgeoisie."
"这些小说在叙事推动力、充满活力和有趣的大量角色阵容、以及对生活几乎所有领域的执着兴趣和检验方面是无与伦比的：省城与都市风俗的对比；银行、出版和工业企业的商业领域；艺术、文学和高雅文化世界；政治和党派阴谋；浪漫爱情在各方面；以及贵族和上层资产阶级之间的复杂社会关系和丑闻。"
来源：Britannica - Honoré de Balzac
