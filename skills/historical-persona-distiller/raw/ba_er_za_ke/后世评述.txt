## 写作强度

"From this point forward Balzac spent much of his time in Paris. To most people he seemed full of exuberant vitality, talkative, jovial and robustious, egoistic, credulous, and boastful. He adopted for his own use the armorial bearings of an ancient noble family with which he had no connection and assumed the honorific particle de. He was avid for fame, fortune, and love but was above all conscious of his own genius."

"Between 1828 and 1834 Balzac led a tumultuous existence, spending his earnings in advance as a dandy and man-about-town. A fascinating raconteur, he was fairly well received in society. But social ostentation was only a relaxation from phenomenal bouts of work—14 to 16 hours spent writing at his table in his white, quasi-monastic dressing gown, with his goose-quill pen and his endless cups of black coffee."

"此时起巴尔扎克大部分时间在巴黎度过。对大多数人来说，他显得充满旺盛活力、健谈、欢乐、粗犷、自私、轻信和自负。他采用了与自己毫无关系的一个古老贵族家庭的纹章，并冠以'德'的尊称。他渴望名声、财富和爱情，但首先意识到自己的天才。"

"1828年至1834年间，巴尔扎克过着喧嚣的生活，提前挥霍收入作为花花公子和社交人物。作为一个迷人的故事讲述者，他在社交界受到相当好的接待。但社交排场只是他从非凡的工作中的放松——每天14到16小时坐在桌前写作，穿着他白色 quasi monastic dressing gown，用他的鹅毛笔和无穷无尽的黑咖啡。"

来源：Britannica

## 人物类型

"Balzac admired those individuals who were ruthless, astute, and, above all, successful in thrusting their way up the social and economic scale at all costs."

"巴尔扎克钦佩那些无情的、精明的、而且不惜一切代价向上爬的人。"

来源：Britannica

---

## 社会分析

"The whole is an examination of French society from the French Revolution to the eve of the Revolution of 1848, in which Balzac analyzed the underlying principles of this constantly developing world. Balzac ranged back and forth, often within the same novel, from the philosophical to the social, the economic, and the legal; from Paris to the provinces; and from the summit of society to the petite bourgeoisie."

"整体是对法国社会从法国大革命到1848年革命前夕的审视，巴尔扎克分析了这一不断发展世界的根本原则。巴尔扎克经常在同一小说中往返于哲学与社会、经济与法律之间；从巴黎到外省；从社会顶层到小资产阶级。"

来源：Britannica - The Human Comedy

## 人生轨迹

"From then on his life was to be one of mounting debts and almost incessant toil. He returned to writing with a new mastery, and his literary apprenticeship was over."

"从此他的一生就是债务不断增加和几乎不停息的劳作。他以新的驾驭力回归写作，文学徒弟期结束。"

来源：Britannica

## 晚年与逝世

"Balzac agreed to marry when her husband died, and so Balzac continued to conduct his courtship of her by correspondence; the resulting Lettres à l'étrangère ('Letters to a Foreigner'), which appeared posthumously (4 vol., 1889–1950), are an important source of information for the history both of Balzac's life and of his work."

"巴尔扎克约定等她丈夫去世后结婚，因此继续通过书信追求她。结果形成的《致外国女子的书信》（四卷，1889-1950年死后出版）是了解巴尔扎克生活和工作历史的重要来源。"

来源：Britannica

---

## 创造力高峰

"To clear his debts and put himself in a position to marry Madame Hanska now became Balzac's great incentive. He was at the peak of his creative power. In the period 1832–35 he produced more than 20 works, including the novels Le Médecin de campagne (1833; The Country Doctor), Eugénie Grandet (1833), L'Illustre Gaudissart (1833; The Illustrious Gaudissart), and Le Père Goriot (1835), one of his masterpieces."

"为了清除债务并为自己与韩斯卡夫人结婚创造条件，成为巴尔扎克的巨大动力。他正处于创造力巅峰。1832-35年期间，他创作了20多部作品，包括《乡村医生》（1833年）、《欧也妮·葛朗台》（1833年）、《著名的戛迪萨尔》（1833年）和《高老头》（1835年），后者是他的杰作之一。"

来源：Britannica
