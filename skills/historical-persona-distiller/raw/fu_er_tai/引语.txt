# 伏尔泰（Voltaire）引语与名言

> 来源：World History Encyclopedia、Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy
> 获取时间：2026-05-01

## 代表性引语

### 关于宗教与宽容

> "Is each individual citizen, then, to be permitted to believe only in what his reason tells him, to think only what his reason, be it enlightened or misguided, may dictate? Yes, indeed he should, provided always that he threatens no disturbance to public order."
> ——《论宽容》（Treatise on Tolerance，1762）

> "Religion is the source of all the follies and turmoils imaginable, it is the mother of fanaticism and civil discord; it is the enemy of mankind."
> ——《哲学词典》（Philosophical Dictionary，1764），引自 Gottlieb 233

### 关于上帝与自然神论

> "Who are you? Where do you come from? What are you doing? What will become of you? This is a question one must put to every creature in the universe, but none of them give us any answer."
> ——《无知的哲学家》（Ignorant Philosopher，1766），引自 Hampson 122

### 关于君主与权力

> "You kings, you put us on our guard, you are like Homer's gods who make men serve their purposes without these poor people suspecting it."
> ——1775年致腓特烈大帝信，引自 Robertson 668

### 关于理性与启蒙

> "Enlightened times will enlighten only a small number of honest people. The vulgar masses will always be fanatics."
> ——致友人信，引自 Gottlieb 233

### 关于乐观主义与里斯本地震

里斯本1755年大地震后，伏尔泰写作《论里斯本的灾难》质疑莱布尼茨的乐观主义：

> "Everything happens for the best" is a dangerous doctrine.
> ——《论里斯本的灾难》（Poème sur le désastre de Lisbonne，1756）

### 关于批判与行动

> "If we recognise the harshness of life, we can do something about it using reason to make practical improvements."
> ——《老实人》（Candide，1759）核心思想

### 关于知识与知识分子

> "We live in a century in which the philosophical spirit has rid us of a great number of prejudices."
> ——引自 Diderot，由 Robertson 27 引用

### 关于专制与自由

> "He argues that different denominations of Christianity should be tolerated in the same society. This is based on his belief that toleration springs from no single group being able to claim absolute truth."
> ——World History Encyclopedia 对《哲学通信》的评价

### 关于政府形式

> "He proposes that the best form of government is a monarchy limited in its power by a parliament and constitution."
> ——World History Encyclopedia

### 关于人性与自利

> "Voltaire believes that because humans are rational and act with reasonable self-interest, they should be given more liberty than current authoritarian government systems allow."
> ——World History Encyclopedia
