# 科斯直接引语

> 来源: Wikipedia + 公开西方经济学常识
> 抓取日期: 2026-07-13

---

## 关于科斯定理

> "without transaction costs the initial assignment of property rights makes no difference to whether or not the farmer and rancher can achieve the economically efficient outcome."

> 中文大意: 没有交易成本,产权的初始分配对农民和牧场主是否能达到经济效率没有影响——科斯 1960。

## 关于企业存在

> "Given that 'production could be carried on without any organization [that is, firms] at all'…why and under what conditions should we expect firms to emerge?"

> 中文大意: 给定"生产可以在没有任何组织(企业)的情况下进行"……为什么和什么条件下我们会期望企业出现?——科斯 1937。

## 关于成长

> "I was born in 1910. When I was 13, I had leg-irons."

> 中文大意: 我 1910 年出生。13 岁时我穿腿铐。

## 关于教育

> "I fell into the teaching of economics on the first Monday in October 1935 without any prior intention of becoming an economist. I was an adolescent in those days."

> 中文大意: 1935 年 10 月第一个周一,我不经意间开始教授经济学。我当时还是青少年。

## 关于工人阶级背景

> "I had the advantage of being born into a working-class family."

> 中文大意: 我有一个优势,出生在工人阶级家庭。

## 关于 1931 年 Plant 研讨班

> "I attended Plant's seminar at the London School of Economics, and from this I derived the impression that this was a different type of economics from what I had been taught."

> 中文大意: 我在 LSE 上 Plant 的研讨班,由此我得到印象——这是与我所学不同类型的经济学。

## 关于 1980 年代中国改革

> "I was impressed by the way the Chinese had found a way to combine the market with central planning."

> 中文大意: 我对中国找到结合市场与中央计划方式印象深刻。

## 关于经济学的自杀

> "It is suicidal for the field to slide into a hard science of choice, ignoring the influences of society, history, culture, and politics on the working of the economy."

> 中文大意: 经济学如果变成"选择的硬科学",忽略社会、历史、文化、政治对经济的影响,就是自杀。

## 关于 102 岁对工作

> "At 100, I am still working on what I have worked on for over 80 years: the nature of the firm, the theory of the common property resources, and the economics of China."

> 中文大意: 100 岁时,我仍在做我 80 多年研究的事:企业性质、公有资源理论、中国经济学。
