# 贝多芬直接引语

> 来源: Wikipedia + 公开西方音乐常识
> 抓取日期: 2026-07-13

---

## 海利根斯塔特遗书(1802)

> "I shall seize Fate by the throat; it shall certainly not bend me completely. It shall not make me a cripple."

> 中文大意: 我将扼住命运的咽喉;它绝不会完全使我屈服。它不会使我成为残废。

## 关于命运

> "I am not satisfied with the work I have done so far. From now on I intend to take a new way."

> 中文大意: 我对迄今的工作不满意。从今我要走新路。

## 关于耳聋

> "Let your deafness no longer be a secret — even in art."

> 中文大意: 让您的耳聋不再是秘密——即使在艺术中(1806)。

## 关于 Rilumovsky 四重奏

> "Oh, they are not for you, but for a later age!"

> 中文大意: 哦,那不是为你,而是为后世——对批评家 Radicati 的话。

## 关于歌德

> "Goethe delights far too much in the court atmosphere, far more than is becoming in a poet."

> 中文大意: 歌德太享受宫廷气氛,远超过诗人应有的。

## 关于歌德(晚期)

> "The admiration, the love and esteem which already in my youth I cherished for the one and only immortal Goethe have persisted."

> 中文大意: 我对唯一不朽歌德在青年时就怀有的钦佩、爱与尊重,至今持续。

## 关于艺术

> "Music is the wine which inspires one to new generative processes."

> 中文大意: 音乐是激发新创造过程的酒。

## 关于艺术使命

> "I will take fate by the throat; it shall not bend me completely."

> 中文大意: 我要扼住命运咽喉;它不会完全使我屈服(写给 Wegeler)。

## 关于永垂不朽

> "Music is a higher revelation than all wisdom and philosophy."

> 中文大意: 音乐是比所有智慧与哲学都更高的启示。

## 关于艺术与救赎

> "I know that I am living, even more, that I have never been carried away by vulgar pleasure, and that I have not fled from death in the practice of virtue. I am fully convinced that I shall find in death not only the possibility of my great happiness, but that of the happiness of all who surround me."
