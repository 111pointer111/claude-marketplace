## 世界史百科评价

"During the later Middle Ages (c. 1300-1500 CE), he was referred to as 'The Master', most notably in Dante's Inferno where the author did not need to even identify Aristotle by name for him to be recognized."

"After the death of Alexander the Great in 323 BCE, when the tide of Athenian popular opinion turned against Macedon, Aristotle was charged with impiety owing to his earlier association with Alexander and the Macedonian Court. With the unjust execution of Socrates in mind, Aristotle chose to flee Athens, 'lest the Athenians sin twice against philosophy', as he said."

"Aristotle's writings, like Plato's, have influenced virtually every avenue of human knowledge for the past two thousand years. Although he was not widely read in the west after the fall of Rome, his works were appreciated in the east where Muslim scholars drew inspiration and understanding from his works."

## 但丁《神曲》中的记载

在但丁的《地狱篇》第四歌中，但丁描写了四位伟大的古代诗人——荷马、贺拉斯、俄维德、卢坎——在"幽狱"（Limbo）中等候，并称亚里士多德为"哲学大师"（il maestro di color che sanno）。

## 斯坦福百科评价

"His thirty-one surviving works (those contained in the 'Corpus Aristotelicum') all contain recognizably Aristotelian doctrine; and most of these contain theses whose basic purport is clear."

"Cicero was arguably the greatest prose stylist of Latin and was also without question an accomplished and fair-minded critic of the prose styles of others writing in both Latin and Greek. We must assume, then, that Cicero had before him works of Aristotle other than those we possess."

"Aristotle's prose requires some acclimatization... Still, many of those who persist with Aristotle come to appreciate the unembellished directness of his style."

## 康德对亚里士多德逻辑学的评价

"'That from the earliest times logic has traveled a secure course can be seen from the fact that since the time of Aristotle it has not had to go a single step backwards… What is further remarkable about logic is that until now it has also been unable to take a single step forward, and therefore seems to all appearance to be finished and complete.'"

——康德《纯粹理性批判》B vii，引自Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy

## 互联网哲学百科评价

"Aristotle is traditionally linked in sequence with Socrates and Plato in the triad of the three greatest Greek philosophers."

"Aristotle was known, in his time, as 'the man who knew everything' and later simply as 'The Philosopher', needing no further qualification as his fame was so widespread."

## 历史地位

亚里士多德的形而上学、逻辑学、伦理学、政治学、诗学几乎影响了人类知识所有领域。他被犹太教、基督教和穆斯林神学广泛吸收和发展。

康德在《纯粹理性批判》中写道："亚里士多德逻辑学自亚里士多德时代以来走的是一条可靠的道路，不需要退后一步；但同样令人注目的是，两千多年后的今天，它也没有向前迈出一步，似乎已经完成。"

## 现代评价

亚里士多德的贡献包括：
- 发明了形式逻辑（syllogistic logic）
- 提出了科学方法论的雏形
- 创立了目的论（teleology）哲学
- 系统化了博物学（natural history）研究
- 创建了政治学作为独立学科
- 奠定了文学批评（诗学）的基础

他的影响延伸至伊斯兰黄金时代、基督教经院哲学、文艺复兴和启蒙运动。
