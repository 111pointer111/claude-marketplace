## Quotes from Biography.com

"Our virtues and our failings are inseparable, like force and matter. When they separate, man is no more."

"I do not think you can name many great inventions that have been made by married men."

"The scientists of today think deeply instead of clearly. One must be sane to think clearly, but one can think deeply and be quite insane."

## Quotes from Tesla Society Biography

"I know two great men, one is you and the other is this young man."
— Charles Batchelor's letter of introduction for Tesla to Thomas Edison

"If anyone can draw on the power, where do we put the meter?"
— J.P. Morgan, withdrawing funding from Wardenclyffe

## Quotes from Britannica

Tesla was a godsend to reporters who sought sensational copy but a problem to editors who were uncertain how seriously his futuristic prophecies should be regarded.

## Quotes from Baidu Encyclopedia

"若把物体的振动和地球的谐振频率正确地结合起来，在几个星期内，就可以造成地动山摇、地面升降。" ——特斯拉，论特斯拉效应

"整个宇宙不过是一座巨大的机器，它既没有生也没有死。人类不能脱离自然秩序而成为例外，人类也像宇宙一样，是一座机器。"
——特斯拉的生命观点

"女性展示出来的心智能力，完全可以与男性的精神成果和成就相媲美。如果这种能力能够代代相传，未来女性的能力肯定会进一步提高。"
——特斯拉的女性观

"战争无论在道德、经济、现实和理论角度都是不正确的。但和大多数科学家一样，当他脱离科学家的角色时，他的情感又使他认为有些情境下的战争也不无道理。"
——特斯拉的战争观

## Quotes from History.com

Edison told Tesla he would pay $50,000 for an improved design for his DC dynamos. After months of experimentation, Tesla presented a solution and asked for the money. Edison demurred, saying, "Tesla, you don't understand our American humor."

## Quotes from Tesla Society - on his mother

His mother Duka Mandic could recite many Serbian epics but had never learned to read or write. She invented many household appliances.

## Tesla's own words (from My Inventions autobiography)

"I am a student of every branch of engineering... the world will wait a long time for Nikola Tesla's equal in achievement and imagination."

## Funeral tributes

"Were we to seize and eliminate from our industrial world the result of Mr. Tesla's work, the wheels of industry would cease to turn, our electric cars and trains would stop, our towns would be dark and our mills would be idle and dead. His name marks an epoch in the advance of electrical science."
— Mr. Behrend, Vice President of Institute of Electrical Engineers (paraphrasing Pope on Newton): "Nature and nature's laws lay hid by night. God said 'Let Tesla be' and all was light."
