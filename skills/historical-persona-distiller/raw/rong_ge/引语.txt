# 卡尔·荣格（Carl Gustav Jung）引语与核心观点
# 来源：Britannica - Carl Jung, collective unconscious, archetype, analytic psychology

## 直接引语与核心概念

### 关于集体无意识
"According to Jung, the collective unconscious contains archetypes, or universal primordial images and ideas."
（荣格认为，集体无意识包含原型，即普遍的原初意象和理念。）

Source: Britannica - collective unconscious

### 关于原型
"In Jung's terms, archetypes are instinctive patterns, have a universal character, and are expressed in behaviour and images."
（在荣格的术语中，原型是本能的模式，具有普遍性，并在行为和图像中表达出来。）

Source: Britannica - Carl Jung biography

### 关于分析心理学
"Analytical psychology, the psychoanalytic method of Swiss psychiatrist Carl Jung as he distinguished it from that of Sigmund Freud. Jung attached less importance than did Freud to the role of sexuality in the neuroses and stressed the analysis of patients' immediate conflicts as being more useful in understanding their problems than the uncovering of childhood conflicts."
（分析心理学是瑞士精神科医生卡尔·荣格区别于西格蒙德·弗洛伊德的精神分析方法。荣格比弗洛伊德更少强调性在神经症中的作用，强调分析患者的即时冲突比揭示童年冲突更有助于理解他们的问题。）

Source: Britannica - analytic psychology

### 关于人格类型
"He differentiated two classes of people according to attitude types: extraverted (outward-looking) and introverted (inward-looking). Later he differentiated four functions of the mind—thinking, feeling, sensation, and intuition—one or more of which predominate in any given person."
（他根据态度类型区分了两类人：外向型（向外看）和内向型（向内看）。后来他进一步区分了心灵的四种功能——思维、情感、感觉和直觉——其中一种或多种可能在任何特定个体中占主导地位。）

Source: Britannica - Carl Jung biography

### 关于情结概念
"He used the now famous term complex to describe such conditions."
（他用现在著名的"情结"一词来描述这种情况。）

Source: Britannica - Carl Jung biography

### 关于与弗洛伊德的分歧
"Jung differed with Freud largely over the latter's insistence on the sexual bases of neurosis. A serious disagreement came in 1912, with the publication of Jung's Wandlungen und Symbole der Libido (Psychology of the Unconscious, 1916), which ran counter to many of Freud's ideas."
（荣格与弗洛伊德的主要分歧在于后者坚持神经症的性基础。1912年，随着荣格的《力比多的变形与象征》出版，严重分歧出现了，该书与弗洛伊德的许多观点相悖。）

Source: Britannica - Carl Jung biography

### 关于红书与个人经历
"As a boy Jung had remarkably striking dreams and powerful fantasies that had developed with unusual intensity. After his break with Freud, he deliberately allowed this aspect of himself to function again and gave the irrational side of his nature free expression."
（作为一个男孩，荣格有着异常强烈的梦想和幻想。在与弗洛伊德决裂后，他有意地让自己的这些方面再次发挥作用，给了他本性中非理性的一面自由表达的空间。）

Source: Britannica - Carl Jung biography
