## 薛定谔方程(1926)

> **iℏ ∂Ψ/∂t = ĤΨ**

> "The world extended in space and time is but our representation."

## 薛定谔的猫(1935)

> "I don't like it, and I'm sorry I ever had anything to do with it."

> 中文大意: "我不喜欢它,很抱歉曾与它有任何关系。"

## 关于意识

> "Consciousness cannot be accounted for in physical terms. For consciousness is absolutely fundamental. It cannot be accounted for in terms of anything else."

> 中文大意: 意识不能用物理术语解释。因为意识是绝对基础的。它不能用其他事物来解释。

## 关于奥义书

> "Their multiplicity is only apparent, in truth there is only one mind. This is the doctrine of the Upanishads."

> 中文大意: 它们的多样性只是表面的,事实上只有一个心智。这是奥义书的教义。

## 《生命是什么?》(1944)

> "An organism's astonishing gift of concentrating a 'stream of order' on itself and thus escaping the decay into atomic chaos — of 'drinking orderliness' from a suitable environment — seems to be connected with the presence of the 'aperiodic solids' we have just been discussing, the chromosomes being, in fact, the most prominent representatives of this class of matter."

> 中文大意: 生命体将"秩序流"集中于自身的能力似乎与"非周期性固体"(染色体)有关——预见了 DNA 作为非周期性晶体的思想。
