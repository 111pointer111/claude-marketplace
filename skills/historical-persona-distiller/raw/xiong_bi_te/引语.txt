## 关于创造性破坏

> "creative destruction is the essential fact about capitalism."

> 中文大意: 创造性破坏是关于资本主义的根本事实。

> "the gale of creative destruction" (《资本主义、社会主义与民主》)

> 中文大意: 创造性破坏的大风。

## 关于大公司

> "the trail leads not to the doors of those firms that work under conditions of comparatively free competition but precisely to the door of the large concerns… a shocking suspicion dawns upon us that big business may have had more to do with creating that standard of life than with keeping it down."

> 中文大意: 道路不是引向在相对自由竞争条件下经营的企业的大门,而是引向大公司的大门……一个令人震惊的怀疑在我们心中浮现——大企业可能与创造生活水平的关系比压低生活水平的关系更密切。

## 关于企业家精神

> "the entrepreneur is never found in the actual strata of society which the practical and the populistic writers on the subject have in mind."

> 中文大意: 企业家从未在社会实际阶层中找到,而这些阶层是实用主义与民粹主义作家所关注的。

> "the function of entrepreneurs is to reform or revolutionize the pattern of production by exploiting an invention or, more generally, an untried technological possibility for producing a new commodity or producing an old one in a new way, by opening up a new source of supply of materials or a new outlet for products, by reorganizing an industry and so on."

> 中文大意: 企业的功能是通过利用一项发明或更普遍地利用一种未经试验的技术可能性,改革或革新生产模式,生产新商品或以新方式生产旧商品,开辟新材料来源或新销售出路,重组行业等。

## 关于民主

> "the theory of democracy is simply that the equipment for arriving at political decisions is supplied to the population by means of recruiting political leaders."

> 中文大意: 民主理论不过是装备给人民通过招募政治领导达成政治决策的机制。

## 关于人生目标

> "three goals in life: to be the greatest economist in the world, the best horseman in all of Austria, and the greatest lover in all of Vienna. He claimed to have reached two of these goals, though he never specified which two."

> 中文大意: 人生三个目标:成为世界最伟大的经济学家,奥地利最好的骑手,维也纳最伟大的情人。他声称达到两个,但从未指明哪两个。

## 关于企业家的职能

> "the innovation and technological change of a nation come from entrepreneurs or wild spirits."

> 中文大意: 一个国家的创新与技术变革来自企业家或"野性精神"。
