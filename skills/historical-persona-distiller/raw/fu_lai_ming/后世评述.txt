# 弗莱明 后世评述

## 科学史地位

- "The single greatest victory ever achieved over disease." — 普遍评价弗莱明青霉素发现

- "It is estimated that penicillin has saved at least 200 million lives since its first use as a medicine in 1942." — New World Encyclopedia

- Fleming在1999年被《时代》杂志列入"20世纪100位最重要人物"

- 2002年BBC"最伟大的100位英国人"投票第23名

- 2009年STV投票评选"最伟大的苏格兰人"第3名（前两位是罗伯特·彭斯和威廉·华莱士）

## 同代人评价

- Sir Almroth Wright: strongly supported Fleming's findings on antiseptics during WWI, but despite this, most army physicians continued to use antiseptics even in cases where this worsened the condition of the patients.

- Sir Henry Harris (1998): "Without Fleming, no Chain; without Chain, no Florey; without Florey, no Heatley; without Heatley, no penicillin."

- V. D. Allison (on Fleming's lab habits): "[If] he had been as tidy as he thought I was, he would not have made his two great discoveries."

- Henry Dale (on Fleming's 1929 presentation): He did not even sense any striking point of importance in Fleming's speech.

- Ernst Chain: Upon learning Fleming was coming to visit Oxford in 1940, reportedly said "Good God! I thought he was dead."

## 弗莱明的自我评价

- Fleming called his fame the "Fleming Myth," humbly recognizing that he was the chosen representative to embody the accomplishments of the many other pioneers who developed antibiotics.

- 他将自己的成功归因于"幸运的意外"（serendipity）

## 历史评价

- 弗莱明发现的溶菌酶（1921）直到20世纪末才被认识到其作为先天免疫 antimicrobial protein 的重要性

- 青霉素在二战期间（1944年D日）已能够生产足够治疗所有盟军伤员

- 弗莱明早在1945年就警告了抗生素耐药性的危险："the ignorant man may easily underdose himself and by exposing his microbes to non-lethal quantities of the drug make them resistant"

## 关于弗莱明的著名误解

- 弗莱明本人否认救了温斯顿·丘吉尔的命（丘吉尔实际是被Sulphapyridine（M&B 693）治好的）。弗莱明称这个故事为"A wondrous fable."

## 荣誉与奖项

- 1943年当选英国皇家学会会员（FRS）
- 1944年与Florey一同被授予骑士称号
- 1945年与Florey、Chain共享诺贝尔生理学或医学奖
- 获得近30所欧美大学荣誉博士学位
- 担任爱丁堡大学的名誉院长（1951-1954）
- 几乎所有医学和科学学会的荣誉会员
- 罗马天主教徒
