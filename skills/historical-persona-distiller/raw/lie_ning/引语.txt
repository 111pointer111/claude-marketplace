## 《四月提纲》(1917-04)

> "Peace, Land, and Bread."

> "All power to the Soviets."

## 关于一战 (1915)

> "being waged for the division of colonies and the robbery of foreign territory."

## 《怎么办?》(1902)核心论断

> "Without a revolutionary theory there can be no revolutionary movement."

> "The proletariat, in virtue of its economic role, can serve as the vanguard of the struggle..."

> "Give us an organization of revolutionaries, and we will overturn the whole of Russia."

## 《国家与革命》(1917)

> "The state is a product and a manifestation of the irreconcilability of class antagonisms."

> "The working class must smash the old state machine and replace it with a new one, the proletarian state."

## 关于先锋党

> "We must have a vanguard of the proletariat — a party capable of directing the masses in the struggle."

## 关于十月革命

> "Insurrection is the highest form of political action."

> "We must speak of the necessity of preparing for insurrection as a practical necessity of the moment."

## 关于马克思主义实践

> "Without revolutionary theory there can be no revolutionary practice."

## 关于资本主义与帝国主义

> "Imperialism is the monopoly stage of capitalism."

> "The highest stage of capitalism is the era of finance capital, the era of bank monopolies, the era of merging of bank capital with industrial capital."

## 关于革命与战争(瑞士时期)

> "The capitalist war of 1914-18 is being waged for the division of colonies and the robbery of foreign territory."

## 关于"封闭列车"

> (列宁当时未公开评论,但其行动选择显示了对"战争变国内战争"战略的极致应用)

## 关于党内民主与派系

> 1921 年第十次代表大会决议:"The Congress... instructs all Party organisations to dissolve all factions and groups formed on lines of factional disagreement..."

## 关于死亡临近(政治遗嘱)

> 警告斯大林"too rude"——列宁口授笔记中对斯大林个人品质的批判

## 引语使用注意

1. 《四月提纲》、《怎么办?》、《国家与革命》是列宁最具影响力的著作,引语可信度高
2. 政治遗嘱笔记是 1922-23 年口授,经后人整理编辑,有些版本差异
3. 关于"封闭列车"的引语较少,主要见于 Krupskaya《Memories of Lenin》回忆
4. 十月革命具体指令引语散见于 Trotsky《History of the Russian Revolution》、Reed《Ten Days That Shook the World》
5. 列宁对马克思的引用经常带有个人发挥,需注意是 Marx 还是 Lenin 原文
