# 黑格尔引语集

## 核心哲学命题

### 关于哲学的本质
> "Philosophy is its own time comprehended in thoughts."
> 哲学是自有其事，内容无外乎对其时代的思想把握。
> ——《法哲学原理》序言（PR: 21），Stanford Encyclopedia引

### 关于理性与现实
> "Was vernünftig ist, das ist wirklich; und was wirklich ist, das ist vernünftig."
> 凡是合乎理性的都是现实的，凡是现实的都是合乎理性的。
> ——《法哲学原理》序言

### 关于哲学与神学
> "philosophy has no other object but God and so is essentially rational theology"
> 哲学的唯一对象是上帝，所以哲学本质上是理性的神学。
> ——柏林讲座（Aes I: 101），Stanford Encyclopedia引

### 关于辩证法
> "Hegel developed a dialectical scheme that emphasized the progress of history and of ideas from thesis to antithesis and thence to a higher and richer synthesis."
> 黑格尔发展了一种辩证图式，强调历史和思想从正题到反题、再到更高综合的进步过程。
> ——Britannica

## 关于历史与精神

### 关于历史的目的论
> "an account that was later taken over by Karl Marx and 'inverted' into a materialist theory of an historical development culminating in communism."
> 这一历史观后来被卡尔·马克思采纳并"颠倒"为一种以唯物主义理论为基础的历史发展理论，最终以共产主义为顶点。
> ——Stanford Encyclopedia

### 关于精神的自我认识
> "The peculiarity of Hegel's form of idealism lies in his idea that the mind of God becomes actual only via its particularization in the minds of 'his' finite material creatures."
> 黑格尔唯心主义的特征在于，他认为上帝的精神只有通过其在大地上的有限生灵的心灵才能成为现实的。
> ——Stanford Encyclopedia

---

## 关于自我意识与承认

### 主奴辩证法核心
> "the account of the struggle of recognition in which Hegel examines the inter-subjective conditions which he sees as necessary for any form of consciousness."
> 关于争取承认的斗争，黑格尔审视了他认为任何形式的意识所必需的相互主体性条件。
> ——Stanford Encyclopedia

> "One's self-consciousness will be dependent on one's recognition of those others as similarly recognizing oneself as a self-conscious subject."
> 个人的自我意识将依赖于他对那些同样将本人承认为自我意识主体的他人的承认。
> ——Stanford Encyclopedia

> "Such complex patterns of mutual recognition constituting objective spirit thereby provide the social matrix within which individual self-consciousnesses can exist as such."
> 这种构成客观精神的相互承认的复杂模式，为个人自我意识的存在提供了社会基础。
> ——Stanford Encyclopedia

---

## 关于伦理实体（Sittlichkeit）

> "Hegel's discussion of spirit starts from what he calls Sittlichkeit (translated as 'ethical order' or 'ethical substance')"
> 黑格尔对精神的讨论从他所称的Sittlichkeit（译为"伦理秩序"或"伦理实体"）出发。
> ——Stanford Encyclopedia

---

## 关于哲学的内容

> "Hegelian philosophy has no distinct doctrine of its own; its content is the right understanding of past attempts at account-giving in their limitations and interconnection."
> 黑格尔哲学没有独特的专属教义；其内容是在其局限与相互关联中，正确理解过去一切试图提供理由说明（account-giving）的努力。
> ——Stanford Encyclopedia，引Pippin (2019)

## 生平自述/同时代人描述

### Britannica记载
> "Hegel was the last of the great philosophical system builders of modern times."
> 黑格尔是现代最后一位伟大的哲学体系建构者。
> ——Britannica

> "As a schoolboy he made a collection of extracts, alphabetically arranged, comprising annotations on classical authors, passages from newspapers, and treatises on morals and mathematics from the standard works of the period."
> 作为一个学生，他按字母顺序编排了一本摘录集，包括对古典作家的批注、报纸文章段落，以及当时标准著作中关于道德和数学的论文。
> ——Britannica

### 关于青年时期与荷尔德林、谢林
> "His chief friends during that period were a pantheistic poet, J.C.F. Hölderlin, his contemporary, and the nature philosopher Schelling, five years his junior. Together they read the Greek tragedians and celebrated the glories of the French Revolution."
> 他当时的主要朋友有一位泛神论诗人荷尔德林（与他同辈）和比他小五岁的自然哲学家谢林。他们一起阅读希腊悲剧作家，庆祝法国大革命的荣光。
> ——Britannica
