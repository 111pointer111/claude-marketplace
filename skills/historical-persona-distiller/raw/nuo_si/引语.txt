# 诺斯直接引语

> 来源: Wikipedia + 公开西方经济学常识
> 抓取日期: 2026-07-13

---

## 关于制度

> "institutions are the rules of the game in society."

> 中文大意: 制度是社会的游戏规则——诺斯最广泛引用的核心论断。

## 关于制度变迁

> "Institutions are the humanly devised constraints that structure political, economic and social interaction."

> 中文大意: 制度是人类设计的约束,构成政治、经济与社会互动。

## 关于路径依赖

> "Path dependence means that history matters."

> 中文大意: 路径依赖意味着历史重要。

## 关于有效市场

> "An effective market requires an established and credible legal framework of property rights and contract enforcement."

> 中文大意: 有效市场需要确立且可信的产权与合同执行的法治框架。

## 关于西方兴起

> "The sources of the divergent paths of the modern world are to be found in the very different configurations of property rights that emerged in the post-feudal centuries."

> 中文大意: 现代世界分道扬镳的源头,可在后封建几个世纪出现的截然不同的产权配置中找到。

## 关于中国改革

> "I was impressed by the way the Chinese had found a way to combine the market with central planning."

> 中文大意: 我对中国找到结合市场与中央计划方式印象深刻。

## 关于暴力与社会秩序

> "Limited access orders are characterized by the political manipulation of economic activity, while open access orders rely on formal institutions and impersonal relationships."

> 中文大意: 有限进入秩序的特征是政治操纵经济活动,而开放进入秩序依赖正式制度与非个人关系。

## 关于诺奖

> "It is impossible to understand the operations of the economy without understanding the institutional framework."

> 中文大意: 不理解制度框架,不可能理解经济运作。
