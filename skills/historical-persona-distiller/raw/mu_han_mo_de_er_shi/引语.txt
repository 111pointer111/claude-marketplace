# 穆罕默德二世直接引语 - Wikiquote

## 关于艺术与城市

> "True art is to create a magnificent city and fill the hearts of its people with happiness." (Fatih Mosque Foundation Charter / Fatih 清真寺基金会宪章)
>
> 中文: "真正的艺术是创造一座宏伟的城市,并让其人民心中充满幸福。"

## 关于征服

> "Tell to your emperor that where my power has reached, not even his dreams can!" (To Byzantine ambassador during siege / 对围攻期间的拜占庭使节)
>
> 中文: "告诉你的皇帝,我的权力所到之处,即使他的梦也到不了!"

> "O, Constantinople! It's either I who'll take you, or you!" (During the conquest / 征服期间)
>
> 中文: "啊,君士坦丁堡!要么我拿下你,要么你拿下我!"

> "In order to see the boundaries of the probabilities, we need to try impossible." (On ships passing by land / 关于从陆地运送船只)
>
> 中文: "为了看到可能性的边界,我们需要尝试不可能。"

> "One night, I shall add your kingdom to my empire."
>
> 中文: "总有一天,我会把你的王国纳入我的帝国。"

> "If you cut even a branch out of my forests, I'd cut your head off!"
>
> 中文: "如果你砍了我森林里哪怕一根树枝,我就砍你的头!"

## 关于敌人

> "We are not afraid of the owl, we are sometimes hawks."
>
> 中文: "我们不害怕猫头鹰,我们有时是鹰。"

> "We conquer hearts, not lands."
>
> 中文: "我们征服人心,不征服土地。"

> "To turn away from the enemy is cowardice. Misfortune is the fate of the enemy." (On Crusaders)
>
> 中文: "背向敌人是懦弱。不幸是敌人的命运。"

> "The Christian land has lost its sword and shields." (On Skanderbeg's death)
>
> 中文: "基督教土地已失去了剑与盾。"

## 关于保密

> "If even a single strand of hair in my beard were to learn a secret of mine, I would cut it from the root."
>
> 中文: "如果我胡须里的一根毛发知道了我的秘密,我会从根部拔掉它。"

## 关于宗教与宽容

> "I declare to the whole world, the Bosnians are under protection. No one can disturb these people nor their churches, nor harm them."
>
> 中文: "我向全世界宣告,波斯尼亚人受保护。无人可扰这些人民与他们的教堂,亦不可伤害他们。"

> "We, the Turks, are faithful Muslims."
>
> 中文: "我们土耳其人是虔诚的穆斯林。"

> "Just as there is one sun in the sky, ideally there should be only one state and religion in the world."
>
> 中文: "正如天空中只有一个太阳,理想上世界上应该只有一个国家和宗教。"

## 关于治理

> "If you are the sultan, be at the head of our army in this difficult day for our state. However, if I am the sultan, I order you to come and command my armies immediately!" (Letter to Murad II, age 12 / 12 岁时致 Murad II 的信)
>
> 中文: "如果您是苏丹,请在国家艰难之日亲领大军。但是,如果我是苏丹,我命令您立刻前来指挥我的军队!"

## 关于加拉塔与贸易

> "Even I accepted that the rituals and rituals of the people of Galata should continue in the same manner as they have always been."
>
> 中文: "连我都接受,加拉塔人民的仪式应一如既往地继续。"

> "Let the Genoese merchants travel and trade freely. We will not take their children to join the Janissary corps nor will we ever use force against those who do not accept our religion."
>
> 中文: "让热那亚商人自由旅行与贸易。我们不会带走他们的孩子加入 Janissary 军团,也永不对不接受我们宗教的人使用武力。"

## 关于圣索菲亚

> "Cover the Christian mosaics with plaster so that the believers will not be disturbed. But do not destroy this masterpiece."
>
> 中文: "用灰泥覆盖基督教马赛克,以免信徒受扰。但不要破坏这一杰作。"

## 关于罗马继承

> "I am astonished that the Italians treat me with hostility and incite the Greeks against me, even though we are of the same origin as the Italians and, like them, I have the right to avenge the blood of Hector on the Greeks."
>
> 中文: "我感到惊讶,意大利人如此敌视我并煽动希腊人反对我——尽管我们与意大利人同源,而且我与他们一样有权在希腊人身上为 Hector 之血复仇。"

## 关于信仰与奋斗

> "These troubles are paved in the way of Allah. As we have the sword of Islam in our hands, if we were to not endure such hardship, it would not be worthy to call ourselves ghazis."
>
> 中文: "这些苦难铺在安拉之路上。当我们手中握有伊斯兰之剑,如果不能忍受这样的艰辛,我们就不配称为 ghazi(信仰战士)。"

## WHE 与 Wikipedia 补充引语

### WHE 引用 Caroline Finkel (现代史学家):

> "His palace provided Sultan Mehmed with seclusion. Here he cultivated an aura of mystery and power, which regulations issued towards the end of his reign were designed to enhance."

### WHE 引用 John Freely (现代史学家):

> "Both the sultan and the greatest of his grand vezirs were men of culture and patrons of the arts, and the Conqueror's court in Istanbul rivalled in its brilliance that of Western princes of the European Renaissance."

### Wikipedia 引用 Saadi(波斯诗人,Mehmed 入 Boukoleon 宫殿时吟诵):

> "The spider is curtain-bearer in the palace of Chosroes, / The owl sounds the relief in the castle of Afrasiyab."
>
> 中文: "蜘蛛是科斯洛伊斯宫殿的帷幕侍者 / 猫头鹰在 Afrasiyab 城堡中奏响更声。"
>
> 含义: 帝国的兴衰无常,波斯帝国已成废墟,Mehmed 在此宣告奥斯曼的崛起

### Wikipedia 引用 Aşıkpaşazade (奥斯曼编年史家):

> "All the conquests of Sultan Mehmed were fulfilled with the seizure of Shkodra."

### Wikipedia 引用 Murad II 时期的"Mehmed 致父亲信"(1444,12 岁):

> "If you are the sultan, come and lead your armies. If I am the sultan, I hereby order you to come and lead my armies."
>
> 注: Wikipedia 注明此信据 17 世纪编年史记载,可能为后世追述
