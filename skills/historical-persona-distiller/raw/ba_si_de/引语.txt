# Louis Pasteur Famous Quotes
# Source: Biography.com (biography.com/scientists/louis-pasteur)

## Quotes by Louis Pasteur

1. "Chance only favors the prepared mind."

2. "The role of the infinitely small appeared to me to be infinitely great."

3. "I still have hope, and if my life is spared, I believe that by analogy and experiment, I will attain to the discovery of a remedy."

4. "The universe is an asymmetrical entity. I am inclined to believe that life as it is manifested to us must be a function of [that] asymmetry."

5. "There is no such thing as a special category of science called applied science. There is science and there are its applications, which are related to one another as the fruit is related to the tree that has borne it."

6. "After death, life reappears in a different form and with different laws."

7. "One must not assume that an understanding of science is present in those who borrow its language."

8. "Life is the germ, and the germ is life."

9. "A known enemy is already half disarmed."

10. "If one boasts of having done 250 experiments, it means that one has done them badly more than 249 times."

11. "The thought that my name might some day awaken in a child's soul the first stirrings of patriotism makes my heart beat faster."

12. "The universe is asymmetric and I am persuaded that life, as it is known to us, is a direct result of the asymmetry of the universe or of its indirect consequences."
