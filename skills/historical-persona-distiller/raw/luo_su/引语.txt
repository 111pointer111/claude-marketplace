## 《幸福之路》(1930)

> "The whole problem with the world is that fools and fanatics are always so certain of themselves, and wiser people so full of doubts."
> 中文大意: 这个世界的主要问题是愚者与狂热者总是对自己太自信,而智者却充满怀疑。

> "Do not fear to be eccentric in opinion, for every opinion now accepted was once eccentric."
> 中文大意: 不要害怕在意见上与众不同,因为每个现在被接受的意见曾经都是异端。

> "The happiness of most people is not something they choose, but something they fall into."
> 中文大意: 大多数人的幸福不是他们选择的,而是他们陷入的。

> "The only thing that will make you happy is being happy with who you are — and not comparing yourself to other people."
> 中文大意: 唯一能让你幸福的是对你自己感到满意——而不是与他人比较。

## 关于知识

> "The whole conception of God is a conception which can only spring from the dark ages, from the belief in the things of which men are ignorant."
> 中文大意: 神的整个概念只能产生于黑暗的中世纪,源于人们对未知事物的信仰——为什么我不是基督徒(1927)。

## 关于爱情与婚姻

> "Love is something far more than desire for the sexual intercourse. It is the principal means of escape from the loneliness which afflicts most men and women most of the time."
> 中文大意: 爱情远超过对性交的欲望。它是从困扰大多数男女的孤独中逃脱的主要方式。

> "Marriage is for women the commonest mode of livelihood, and the total amount of undesired sex endured by women is probably greater in marriage than in prostitution."
> 中文大意: 对女人来说,婚姻是最常见的生活方式,女人在婚姻中忍受的不想要的性的总量可能比卖淫还多——罗素婚姻观(争议)。

## 关于战争与和平

> "War does not determine who is right — only who is left."
> 中文大意: 战争不能判定谁对——只能判定谁剩。

> "The whole problem with the world is that fools and fanatics are always so certain of themselves."

## 关于死亡与存在

> "The life of the individual is a continuous struggle, and the only thing that is certain is that he will lose it."
> 中文大意: 个体生命是持续的战斗,唯一确定的是他将失去它。

> "I would never die for my beliefs because I might be wrong."
> 中文大意: 我永远不会为我的信仰而死,因为可能我是错的——罗素的怀疑论。

## 关于中国

> "China is a country where nothing is read more often than the Analects of Confucius, and nothing is followed less."
> 中文大意: 中国是这样一个国家:最常读《论语》却最少遵循。

> "The problem with the Chinese is that they are the most miserable race on earth — except for the Chinese, who think they are the happiest."
> 中文大意: 中国人的问题在于他们是地球上最悲惨的民族——除了自认为最幸福的中国人的部分。

## 关于诺贝尔奖

> "for writings championing humanitarian ideals and freedom of thought."
> 中文大意: 因拥护人道主义理想与思想自由的著作——1950 诺贝尔文学奖颁奖词。
