## 信息论(1948)

> "The fundamental problem of communication is that of reproducing at one point either exactly or approximately a message selected at another point."

> 中文大意: 通信的根本问题是:在一个点精确或近似地复制在另一点选择的消息。

> "Information theory has, in the last few years, become something of a scientific bandwagon."

> 中文大意: 信息论在最近几年已成为一种科学潮流——香农在 1956 警告。

> "I just wondered how things were put together."

> 中文大意: 我只是想知道事情是如何组合的——香农对发明的态度。

## Shannon 最大化原则

> "The enemy knows the system."

> 中文大意: 敌人知道系统(Kerckhoffs 原则)。

## 关于科学

> "Information is the resolution of uncertainty."

> 中文大意: 信息是不确定性的解决。

## 关于个人

> "I always loved doing things in new ways. I had a great curiosity about things that other people didn't think about."

> 中文大意: 我一直喜欢以新方式做事。我对别人不思考的事物有强烈好奇心。

> "I'm very fortunate in the things that happened to me."

> 中文大意: 我对发生在我身上的事非常幸运。

## 关于创造

> "Some people build bridges, some build buildings. I build theorems."

> 中文大意: 有些人建桥,有些人建房。我建定理——数学家本性。

## 顽皮语录

> "I don't understand anything that I haven't directly experienced."

> 中文大意: 我没直接经验的东西我都不理解——费曼式的体验主义。

## 杂耍与独轮车

> "I've never been particularly good at remembering things, so I had to invent things to help me remember them."

> 中文大意: 我对记事情从来不擅长,所以我必须发明东西来帮助我记忆。
