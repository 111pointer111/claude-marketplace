URL: https://www.worldhistory.org/homer/

1. "Homer (c. 750 BCE) is perhaps the greatest of all epic poets and his legendary status was well established by the time of Classical Athens."
2. "He composed (not wrote, since the poems were created and transmitted orally, they were not written down until much later) two major works, the Iliad and the Odyssey."
3. "The poems were first compiled, organised, and edited under the Athenian Peisistratus."
4. "The Greeks thought Homer was from Chios or Ionia and credited him with both the Iliad and the Odyssey, the two masterpieces of Greek literature."
5. "The Iliad is universally acclaimed as a truly great story. There is war and gore, honour and betrayal, pain and pathos, love and hatred."

### 来源4：百度百科·荷马
URL: https://baike.baidu.com/item/荷马/84187

1. "荷马（古希腊语：Ὅμηρος，约前9世纪—前8世纪），相传为古希腊的吟游诗人，生于小亚细亚，失明，创作了史诗《伊利亚特》和《奥德赛》，两者统称《荷马史诗》。"
2. "这部史诗也表现了人文主义的思想，肯定了人的尊严、价值和力量。"
3. "1795年，德国学者沃尔夫沿着这一思路对史诗进行了细致的研究，从而断言，史诗的每一部分都曾作为独立的诗歌由歌手们演唱，后经多次整理加工，它才成为我们今天看到的样子。"
