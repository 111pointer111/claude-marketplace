**URL**: https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/hegel/

**引用1**：辩证法三元结构

**引用2**：黑格尔的历史目的论与马克思继承

**引用3**：主奴辩证法
> "the account of the struggle of recognition in which Hegel examines the inter-subjective conditions which he sees as necessary for any form of consciousness."

**引用4**：自我意识依赖于承认
> "One's self-consciousness will be dependent on one's recognition of those others as similarly recognizing oneself as a self-conscious subject."

**引用5**：哲学是对其时代的思想把握
> "Philosophy is its own time comprehended in thoughts." (PR: 21)

**引用6**：绝对唯心论的特征
> "The peculiarity of Hegel's form of idealism lies in his idea that the mind of God becomes actual only via its particularization in the minds of 'his' finite material creatures."

**引用7**：哲学的唯一对象是上帝
> "philosophy 'has no other object but God and so is essentially rational theology'" (Aes I: 101)

**引用8**：主奴辩证法详情
> "Such complex patterns of mutual recognition constituting objective spirit thereby provide the social matrix within which individual self-consciousnesses can exist as such."

**引用9**：Sittlichkeit（伦理实体）

**引用10**：哲学本质
> "Hegelian philosophy has no distinct doctrine of its own; its content is the right understanding of past attempts at account-giving in their limitations and interconnection."
