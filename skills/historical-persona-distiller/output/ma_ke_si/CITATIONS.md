### 15. Four dimensions of alienated labour (1844 Manuscripts)
- **原文**: "In a much-discussed passage from the 1844 Manuscripts, Marx identifies four dimensions of alienated labour in contemporary capitalist society: (1) immediate producers are separated from the product of their labour; (2) immediate producers are separated from their productive activity; (3) immediate producers are separated from other individuals; (4) immediate producers are separated from their own human nature."
- **出处**: Economic and Philosophic Manuscripts of 1844
- **验证来源**: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy (Section 1.2)
- **原始文本位置**: raw/ma_ke_si/传记.txt (Stanford Encyclopedia section)

### 20. On Marx's death and burial
- **原文**: "Marx died of pleurisy in London on March 14, 1883. While his original grave had only a nondescript stone, the Communist Party of Great Britain erected a large tombstone, including a bust of Marx, in 1954. The stone is etched with the last line of The Communist Manifesto ('Workers of all lands unite'), as well as a quote from the Theses on Feuerbach."
- **出处**: Biography.com
- **原始文本位置**: raw/ma_ke_si/传记.txt (Biography.com section)
