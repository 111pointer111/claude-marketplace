# 达尔文原文引用清单

## 思想内核引用

### 自然选择机制
- "any advantage, however slight, over its competitors will lead to more offspring in the long run, and hence the accumulation of advantageous change"
  来源：IEP Darwin词条 (raw/da_er_wen/后世评述_iep.txt)

- "The extinction of species and of whole groups of species, which has played so conspicuous a part in the history of the organic world, almost inevitably follows from the principle of natural selection."
  来源：Biography.com (raw/da_er_wen/传记_biography.txt)

### 生存斗争
- "Every organism is locked in a constant battle to survive and reproduce, whether with other members of its species, other species, or even its environmental conditions (of drought or temperature, for instance)"
  来源：IEP Darwin词条 (raw/da_er_wen/后世评述_iep.txt)

- "Multiply, vary, let the strongest live and the weakest die."
  来源：Biography.com (raw/da_er_wen/传记_biography.txt)

### 共同祖先与进化
- "from so simple a beginning endless forms most beautiful and most wonderful have been, and are being, evolved"
  来源：《物种起源》结语，引用于Biography.com (raw/da_er_wen/传记_biography.txt)

- "the species we know today had gradually evolved from common ancestors"
  来源：Biography.com (raw/da_er_wen/传记_biography.txt)

### 科学方法论
- "the largest possible basis of empirical data and generalize from them only when a theoretical claim has received sufficient evidential support"
  来源：IEP Darwin词条，描述培根式归纳法对达尔文的影响 (raw/da_er_wen/后世评述_iep.txt)

### 谨慎的宗教立场
- "I see no good reason why the views given in this volume should shock the religious feelings of anyone."
  来源：《物种起源》，引用于Biography.com (raw/da_er_wen/传记_biography.txt)
