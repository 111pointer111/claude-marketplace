# 孟德尔引语清单 (CITATIONS.md)

## 直接引语

### 1. "My time will come."
> Mendel reported that he told a friend, Gustav von Niessl, when his work was ignored by the scientific community.
> 
> **来源**: Wikipedia — "Mendel reported that he told a friend, Gustav von Niessl: 'My time will come.'"  
> **可信度**: 高 — Wikipedia 明确记载，来源于孟德尔与 Niessl 的通信记录

### 2. "perpetual anxiety about a means of livelihood"
> As the son of a struggling farmer, the monastic life, in his words, spared him the "perpetual anxiety about a means of livelihood."
> 
> **来源**: Wikipedia — citing Robin Marantz Henig, *The Monk in the Garden* (2000), p.  
> **可信度**: 高 — 原始引语，见于 Henig 的孟德尔传记

### 3. "my dearest little animals"
> Mendel, on the other hand, was fond of his bees and referred to them as "my dearest little animals."
> 
> **来源**: Wikipedia — citing Matalova & Kabelka (1982), *Casopis Moravskeho Musea*  
> **可信度**: 高 — 有原始文献记录

### 4. "My scientific studies have afforded me great gratification; and I am convinced that it will not be long before the whole world acknowledges the results of my work."
> **来源**: Biography.com — 孟德尔原话  
> **可信度**: 中 — 见于 Biography.com，为孟德尔的公开陈述

### 5. "The data of most, if not all, of the experiments have been falsified to agree closely with Mendel's expectations."
> **来源**: Ronald Fisher, 1936, *Annals of Science* — 对孟德尔数据的批评性分析  
> **可信度**: N/A — 这是他人对孟德尔的评论，非孟德尔本人言论

### 6. "One can applaud the lucky gambler; but when he is lucky again tomorrow, and the next day, and the following day, one is entitled to become a little suspicious."
> **来源**: A.W.F. Edwards — 对孟德尔悖论的评论  
> **可信度**: N/A — 学者对 Fisher 分析的再评论
