# CITATIONS.md — 麦克斯韦 原文引用清单

## 直接引语（全部可溯源至原始文本）

### 来自 Britannica（James Clerk Maxwell Biography）

1. **爱因斯坦1931年评价（百年诞辰）**
   > "In 1931, on the 100th anniversary of Maxwell's birth, Einstein described the change in the conception of reality in physics that resulted from Maxwell's work as 'the most profound and the most fruitful that physics has experienced since the time of Newton.'"
   — 来源：Britannica - James Clerk Maxwell Biography

2. **霍普金斯评价**
   > "The most extraordinary man he had ever met, that it seemed impossible for him to think wrongly on any physical subject, but that in analysis he was far more deficient."
   — 来源：Britannica（引自Maxwell的剑桥数学老师William Hopkins）

3. **Maxwell婚姻描述**
   > "The union was childless and was described by his biographer as a 'married life…of unexampled devotion.'"
   — 来源：Britannica

4. **母亲去世**
   > "His mother died in 1839 from abdominal cancer, the very disease to which Maxwell was to succumb at exactly the same age."
   — 来源：Britannica

5. **早年论文**
   > "His first scientific paper, published when he was only 14 years old, described a generalized series of oval curves that could be traced with pins and thread by analogy with an ellipse."
   — 来源：Britannica

### 来自 MacTutor History of Mathematics

6. **三岁描写**
   > "He is a very happy man, and has improved much since the weather got moderate; he has great work with doors, locks, keys etc., and 'Show me how it doos' is never out of his mouth. He also investigates the hidden course of streams and bell-wires, the way the water gets from the pond through the wall..."
   — 来源：MacTutor，引自Campbell & Garnett传记（1834年信）

7. **Maxwell学校转变**
   > "About the middle of his school career however he surprised his companions by suddenly becoming one of the most brilliant among them, gaining prizes and sometimes the highest prizes for scholarship, mathematics, and English verse."
   — 来源：MacTutor，引自Tait的Proceedings of the Royal Society of Edinburgh文章

8. **Airy评价土星环论文**
   > "It is one of the most remarkable applications of mathematics to physics that I have ever seen."
   — 来源：MacTutor，引自Airy评价Maxwell的Adams Prize论文

9. **Maxwell论光=电磁波**
   > "We can scarcely avoid the conclusion that light consists in the transverse undulations of the same medium which is the cause of electric and magnetic phenomena."
   — 来源：MacTutor，原文出自Maxwell的电磁学研究

10. **Stokes称赞三原色实验**
    > "I have just received your papers on the dynamical top, etc., and the account of experiments on the perception of colour. The results afford most remarkable and important evidence in favour of the theory of three primary colour-perceptions, a theory which you, and you alone, as far as I know, have established on an exact numerical basis."
    — 来源：MacTutor，引自Stokes 1857年11月7日致Maxwell信

11. **Maxwell土星环信件（1857年8月28日）**
    > "I have effected several breaches in the solid ring, and now am splash into the fluid one, amid a clash of symbols truly astounding. When I reappear it will be in the dusky ring, which is something like the siege of Sebastopol conducted from a forest of guns 100 miles one way, and 30,000 miles the other, and the shot never to stop, but go spinning away round a circle, radius 170,000 miles..."
    — 来源：MacTutor，引自Maxwell 1857年8月28日致Lewis Campbell信

12. **Thomson描述Maxwell在剑桥**
    > "The impression of power which Maxwell produced on all he met was remarkable; it was often much more due to his personality than to what he said, for many found it difficult to follow him in his quick changes from one subject to another, his lively imagination started so many hares that before he had run one down he was off on another."
    — 来源：MacTutor，引自J J Thomson的James Clerk Maxwell Commemorative Volume文章

13. **Fleming描述Maxwell教学**
    > "Maxwell in short had too much learning and too much originality to be at his best in elementary teaching. For those however who could follow him his teaching was a delight."
    — 来源：MacTutor，引自Fleming的Some memories文章

14. **Fleming描述Maxwell最后讲座**
    > "To have enjoyed even a brief personal acquaintance with Professor Maxwell and the privilege of his oral instruction was in itself a liberal education, nay more, it was an inspiration, because everything he said or did carried the unmistakable mark of a genius which compelled not only the highest admiration but the greatest reverence as well."
    — 来源：MacTutor，引自Fleming的Some memories文章

15. **Maxwell临终**
    > "No man ever met death more consciously or more calmly."
    — 来源：MacTutor，引自Dr Paget（Maxwell的医生）

16. **Maxwell健康恶化**
    > "During the last term in May 1879 Maxwell's health evidently began to fail, but he continued to give his lectures up to the end of the term."
    — 来源：MacTutor，引自Fleming的Some memories

### 来自 Britannica（Maxwell's Equations）

17. **麦克斯韦方程组描述**
    > "Maxwell's equations, four equations that, together, form a complete description of the production and interrelation of electric and magnetic fields."
    — 来源：Britannica - Maxwell's Equations

18. **麦克斯韦对安培定律的扩展**

    — 来源：Britannica - Maxwell's Equations
