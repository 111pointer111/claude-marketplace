## 核心誓言引用（来源：Britannica — Hippocratic Oath）

### 1. 师道传承

> "I will reckon him who taught me this Art equally dear to me as my parents, to share my substance with him, and relieve his necessities if required."

**出处**：Hippocratic Oath, Francis Adams translation, 1849; via Britannica

---

### 2. 不伤害原则

> "I will follow that system of regimen which, according to my ability and judgment, I consider for the benefit of my patients, and abstain from whatever is deleterious and mischievous."

**出处**：同上

---

### 3. 禁用毒药

> "I will give no deadly medicine to any one if asked, nor suggest any such counsel."

**出处**：同上

---

### 4. 结石病专科边界

> "I will not cut persons laboring under the stone, but will leave this to be done by men who are practitioners of this work."

**出处**：同上

---

### 5. 保护患者隐私

> "Whatever, in connection with my professional practice or not, in connection with it, I see or hear, in the life of men, which ought not to be spoken of abroad, I will not divulge, as reckoning that all such should be kept secret."

**出处**：同上

### 7. 四体液理论

> "The human body also consisted of four fluids or 'humors' - black bile, yellow bile, phlegm, and blood - as well as four elemental conditions (hot, cold, dry, and moist). Health was associated, according to the great physician, with a balance of all these elements."

**出处**：PMC11526839, Introduction

### 9. 行走是最好的药物

### 10. 整体论

### 11. 脑是意识中枢
