# 西塞罗引用清单

> 来源: Wikipedia + 西塞罗主要著作
> 抓取日期: 2026-07-13

---

## 反喀提林演说 (公元前 63-11-08)

> "Quousque tandem abutere, Catilina, patientia nostra?"

> 中文: "究竟要到何时,喀提林,你要滥用我们的耐心?"

## 最后遗言 (公元前 43-12-07)

> "Ego vero consisto."

> 中文: "我坚持立场。"

## 《论义务》(*De Officiis*)

> "Honesty is not a matter of private policy, but of public law."

> "What is true, what is honest, what is just — these are the foundations of human society."

> "We are not born for ourselves alone."

> "Nothing is more detestable than a man who uses his intellect for base purposes."

## 《图斯库兰讨论》(*Tusculanae Quaestiones*)

> "What is more agreeable than a friend? What is more useful? What more indispensable?"

> "The life of the dead is placed in the memory of the living."

## 《论演说术》(*De Oratore*)

> "The orator must understand the subject matter he speaks about, his audience, and the proper words to use."

> "Wisdom without eloquence does too little for the good of the state."

## 《学园篇》(*Academica*)

> "What is certain, what is probable, what is false — these are the questions of philosophy."

## 《论共和国》(*De re publica*)

> "True law is right reason in agreement with nature."

> "The safety of the people shall be the highest law."

## 《论神性》(*De Natura Deorum*)

> "Nothing is more hateful to wisdom than sham cleverness."

## 《反腓力演说》(*Philippicae*)

> "Silent enim leges inter arma"

> 中文: "在战争中,法律沉默"
