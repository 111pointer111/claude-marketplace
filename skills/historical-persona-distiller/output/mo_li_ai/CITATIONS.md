### 3. Britannica 大英百科全书

**引用9**：
> "Although the sacred and secular authorities of 17th-century France often combined against him, the genius of Molière finally emerged to win him acclaim. Comedy had a long history before Molière, who employed most of its traditional forms, but he succeeded in inventing a new style that was based on a double vision of normal and abnormal seen in relation to each other—the comedy of the true opposed to the specious, the intelligent seen alongside the pedantic."

- **来源**：Britannica - Molière biography（https://www.britannica.com/biography/Moliere-French-dramatist）

- **用途**：莫里哀的戏剧观

**引用11**：

- **来源**：Britannica - Molière biography
- **用途**：描述莫里哀的竞争意识

**引用12**：
